Crit 2048: The Ultimate Math Roguelike & Dungeon Crawler

Crit 2048 is an innovative puzzle game designed for all ages, from kids learning math through merging numbers to adults seeking deep roguelike tactical combat. It combines the addictive mechanics of 2048 with the intensity of a dungeon crawler.

Educational Fun for Kids

Parents and teachers recommend Crit 2048 as a fun way to practice mental math and strategic thinking. The merging mechanics (2+2=4, 4+4=8) teach binary progression and resource management in a safe, ad-free fantasy environment. It is a family-friendly game with no microtransactions.

Deep Strategy for Adults

For fans of Slay the Spire, Balatro, and traditional roguelikes, Crit 2048 offers a punishing and rewarding experience. Build your character class (Wizard, Barbarian, Rogue, etc.), roll a D20 to determine your fate, and forge custom content packs using the built-in Forge modding engine. Crit 2048 is 100% data-driven and community-powered.
